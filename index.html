Gender Inequality Facts

American society has made undeniable improvements in women’s rights in the latest decades and the importance of these changes should not be underestimated. However, that does not mean women have been freed from the burdens of gender inequality. In order to support informed conversations about this topic, we selected a few common arguments and data to help us understand the progress we made and the work we still have left to do.

Below you can find common comments about gender inequality. To see information about each of those comments, hover over them. To learn even more, click to see further information.
